Our Story
About the Hall

Shri Mathoshree Sanganabassamma Ravajappa Madari Kalyana Mantapa was built in sacred memory of the revered Smt. Sanganabassamma and Sri Ravajappa Madari — a tribute to their legacy of love, devotion, and family.

Located in the heart of Muddebihal, Vijayapura District, this magnificent hall has been the backdrop to countless precious family milestones across generations — weddings, Nischitarthas, Namakaranas, Upanayanas, and grand receptions.

Managed today with pride and warmth by Parashuram Madari and Muttanna Madari, the Mantapa beautifully blends Karnataka's rich Veerashaiva traditions with modern infrastructure — making it the most trusted celebration venue in Muddebihal. Rated 4.3★ on JustDial with 94 reviews.
